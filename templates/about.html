Driven by Purpose, Fueled by Community

AutoSeek is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit on a mission to make reliable transportation affordable for everyone—while reinvesting every dollar back into the communities we serve.

Our Mission

AutoSeek Foundation is a California‑based 501(c)(3) committed to vehicle equity, sustainable mobility, and transportation access for underserved communities.  We believe safe, affordable vehicles are not a luxury — they are gateways to economic opportunity, education, and dignity. By researching salvage‑to‑rebuilt processes, promoting circular‑economy policy, and equipping low‑income buyers with practical knowledge, we turn overlooked cars & people into second‑chance success stories.

What We Do

Salvage‑to‑Rebuilt Vehicle Programs

Research & education that turn salvage‑title cars into DMV‑certified, road‑safe vehicles.

Mobility Education & Outreach

Open‑access toolkits, guides, and workshops that demystify vehicle ownership for vulnerable populations.

Workforce Development & STEM

Partnering with colleges & vocational schools to train the next generation of ESG‑minded auto technicians.

Environmental Advocacy

Driving circular‑economy policy and reducing automotive waste through sustainable reuse models.
