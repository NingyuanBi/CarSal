Driven by Purpose, Fueled by Community

AutoSeek is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit on a mission to make reliable transportation affordable for everyone—while reinvesting every dollar back into the communities we serve.

Our Mission

We bridge the gap between unused vehicles and families who need them. By transforming donated cars into affordable options—or the funds that power our programs—we provide sustainable mobility, spur economic opportunity, and reduce waste.
